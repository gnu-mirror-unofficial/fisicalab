12.25 Absolute velocity

Element to measure the absolute velocity of a mobile with circular movement that turns around an element center.

Equations:2
Data:
Object:Name of the mobile with circular movement.
angR:Angle of rotation of the mobile, measured from the positive X axis. The positive sense is the opposite of clockwise.
CenterName of the center.
v:Absolute velocity of the mobile.
ang:Angle of the vector absolute velocity, measured from the positive X axis. The positive sense is the opposite of clockwise.
