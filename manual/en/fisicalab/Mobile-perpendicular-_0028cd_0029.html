12.6 Mobile with perpendicular circular movement

Mobile with circular movement in an horizontal plane. This is, perpendicular to the chalkboard’s plane. Allows apply forces and frictions.

Equations:3
Data:
Name:Object name.
m:Mass.
vt:Tangential velocity of the mobile.
r:Turning radius.
at:Tangential acceleration.
Ft:Applied tangential force.
C:Location of the center. By defaults is ">", meaning that the center is at right. While "<" mean that the center is at left.
