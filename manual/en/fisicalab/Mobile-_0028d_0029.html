10.2 Mobile

Mobile to collision problems. The angles are measured from the positive X axis, and the positive sense is the opposite of clockwise.

Equations:None.
Data:
Name:Name of the mobile.
m:Mass of the mobile.
vi:Initial velocity, in the direction specified by the initial angle.
angi:Initial angle.
vf:Final velocity, in the direction specified by the final angle.
angf:Final angle.
