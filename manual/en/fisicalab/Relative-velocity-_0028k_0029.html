2.12 Relative velocity

Measured the vector relative velocity (magnitude and angle) of the Object 1 relative to Object 2. The angles are measured from the positive X axis, and the positive sense is the opposite of the clockwise.

Equations:2
Data:
Object 1:Object that relative velocity you want.
Object 2:Object reference to measured the relative velocity.
v:Magnitude of the vector relative velocity.
ang:Angle of the vector relative velocity.
