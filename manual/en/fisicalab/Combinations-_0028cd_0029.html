12.27 Supported combinations of elements Energy, Angular Momentum, Linear Momentum and Power

The following abbreviations are used:



OR

Object in rest.

ML

Mobile with linear movement.

MC

Mobile with circular movement.

MP

Mobile with polar circular movement.

C

Center of rotation.

S

Spring.
