10.14 Relative motion

Allows get the acceleration, final velocity and travelled distance of a block in reference to the stationary system. When its data is relative to another block, that making a mobile reference system. The angles are measured from the positive X axis, and the positive sense is the opposite of clockwise.

Equations:6
Data:
Object:Name of the object that its data is relative.
asf:Magnitude of the acceleration.
ang_asf:Angle of the acceleration vector.
vf_sf:Magnitude of the final velocity.
ang_vfsf:Angle of the final velocity vector.
dsf:Travelled distance of the block.
ang_dsf:Angle of the travelled distance.
